Welcome to CrowdNFT!

Our project is a web app that serves as a marketplace for NFTs. Except instead of unique bidders competing against each other, our app will allow users to pool their money together and make bids to purchase that NFT communally. Users will send their funds to a contract, and the contract will be programmed to make bids on their behalf. If the bid is won, then that programmable contract will take ownership of the NFT and distribute a token (equivalent to a share in a public company) representing ownership of that NFT. On the other hand, the contract will refund all funds to their respective owners if it fails to win the auction. The project is inspired by an autonomous contract that was used to bid on a sale of a copy of the US constitution. By using decentralized networks, we hope to enable public ownership of assets with no potentially untrustworthy intermediaries; all decisions will be made via democratic voting.
